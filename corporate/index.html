How Open Insights Operates

One framework. Three partners. A fully traceable analytical chain.

Open Insights is a collaborative effort between SESIT at the University of Victoria, Energy Modelling Hub, and Macrocosm — each committed to building a more transparent and accessible Canadian modelling ecosystem. Open Insights makes these collaborative efforts visible, from academic model design to operational execution, and final assessment publication.

Model Developers

Academic Partners

Energy system models are developed and maintained by academic partners — including researchers at the University of Victoria (SESIT) and Simon Fraser University (EMRG). These teams design the core modelling architecture and advance the underlying methods.

Infrastructure

Energy Modelling Hub

The Energy Modelling Hub operates and maintains the modelling infrastructure, including the M3 Platform. It runs the models under the direction of scientific partners and handles day-to-day operational delivery.

The Framework

Open Insights

Open Insights is the shared framework under which academic model developers and operational infrastructure partners collaborate. OI defines the transparency standards, open-source commitments, and publication processes that govern every assessment and project, ranging from model design to published results.

Academic partners build open-source modelling tools → EMH operates and hosts the infrastructure → Open Insights publishes transparent assessments and documentation for the public.
